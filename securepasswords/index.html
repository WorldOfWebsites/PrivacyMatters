Enter Diceware

You can make a secure, random password easily. First off, get out of the password mentality, instead, think of passphrase since it encourages you to make a longer password. The method that is used is called Diceware.

You will need:

  • A die
  • A copy of the Diceware list, get it here

That's all you need. The list contains 7776 words. Go ahead and open the Diceware word list in a new tab.

There are several implementaions of Diceware for the computer. If you know what you're doing, and are interested, take a look at these ones.

How it Works

By now, the Dicware word list should be open. In the list, each word coressponds to a 5 digit number. You would take a die, and roll it 5 times, writing down each number you just rolled. So, lets say the first number that was rolled is 55366. I will look it up, and in this case, the word is stage. That's the first word in the passphrase. Continue this process as many times as you like.

At this time, 7 words are recommended for the length of the passphrase. If you want some protection against advesaries of the future, go for more words.
